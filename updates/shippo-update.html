The Basics

What is Shippo?

Think of Shippo as your shipping command center. Instead of going to the USPS or UPS website every time you need to ship a ring, everything happens right inside your Rebirth World dashboard.

📦

Real Carrier Rates

Your customers see actual USPS, UPS, and FedEx prices at checkout — not made-up numbers. The price they pay is the real cost to ship from Haleiwa.

🏷

Print Labels From Your Dashboard

When an order comes in, you click one button to buy a shipping label. It gives you a PDF — print it, stick it on the box, drop it off.

📍

Automatic Tracking Updates

Once a package ships, Shippo tells your website when it's in transit, out for delivery, or delivered. Your customer sees this in real-time on their order page.

✅

Smart Packaging

The system knows a skateboard ring is tiny and an apparel order is bigger. It picks the right box size so you're never overpaying for shipping.

The Flow

How a Shipment Works Now

From the moment a customer checks out to the moment their ring arrives — here's what happens behind the scenes.

1

Customer Adds to Cart

They pick their ring, choose a size, maybe add an engraving. On the cart page, they can enter their zip code and instantly see what shipping will cost them — USPS Ground for $5.89, Priority for $14, etc.

2

They Check Out

At checkout (Stripe), they pick their preferred shipping speed. If their order is $100+, they get free standard shipping automatically. They pay, and the order lands in your dashboard.

3

You Buy a Label (One Click)

In your admin dashboard, open the order. Click "Get Rates" to see what carriers are charging. Click "Buy Label" — Shippo purchases the label and gives you a PDF with the barcode, tracking number, everything.

4

You Ship It

Print the label, put it on the package, drop it at USPS or schedule a pickup. Click "Fulfill Order" in the dashboard — your customer gets an email with their tracking number.

5

Tracking Updates Automatically

Shippo watches the package. When it's in transit, delivered, or if there's a problem — your website knows. The customer's order page updates in real-time. You don't have to do anything.

Need Your Input

Questions for You

A few things we need your call on before we flip this live. Tap any question to see why it matters.

01 Is $100 the right threshold for free shipping?
Right now, orders of $100+ get free standard shipping automatically. This means a customer buying 4 skateboard rings ($100) ships free, but 3 rings ($75) doesn't. We can change this to any amount — $75, $50, or remove it entirely.
02 Do you want to offer international shipping at launch?
The system supports it — we have customs declarations built in for shipments outside the US. Currently enabled for CA, GB, AU, NZ. International rates will be higher (USPS Priority International, etc). Do you want this on from day one or start US-only?
03 What carriers do you want to show customers?
Right now we show USPS and UPS options. We can filter to USPS-only if you prefer (simpler for customers, and USPS is usually cheapest for small items from Hawaii). Or we can add FedEx too. Your call.
04 Are the product weights accurate enough?
We're using 4oz for rings and 8oz for apparel as defaults. If the actual weights are different, shipping rates could be slightly off. Do you know the real weights? Even a rough number helps — we can update them in Stripe product metadata.
05 Do you have packaging supplies ready?
The system assumes you're using small jewelry boxes (6" x 4" x 2") for rings and poly mailers for apparel. If you're using different packaging, let us know the dimensions so the rates are accurate. Also — do you have a label printer or are you printing on regular paper?
06 Want USPS pickup from your workshop, or drop-off?
USPS offers free package pickup if you schedule it through their website. Otherwise, you'd drop packages at the Haleiwa post office. This doesn't change anything in the system — just something to plan for your workflow.
