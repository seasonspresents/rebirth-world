HOW I DO IT —
AND WHAT MAKES
IT DIFFERENT

🛹
Born from Skateboard Culture
I've been skating since I was 8. Every skateboard ring starts with a broken deck donated by the local community. The culture isn't a marketing angle — it's who I am.
🏔️
Austrian Master Jeweler Lineage
My father apprenticed under the jeweler who crafted for the Austrian emperor — six years of old-world technique. That standard is in everything I make.
🌊
Inspired by Many Cultures
Hawaii shaped my philosophy. Guatemala deepened my purpose through service with the Mayan women of Lake Atitlán. Solo travels through Latin America broadened my perspective. Made in Utah. Shaped by everywhere.
🌿
Materials With History
Irish bog oak preserved for thousands of years. Stabilized exotic woods from around the world. Recycled skate decks from the local community. Your piece has a past as rich as yours.
💛
Service at the Core
I collaborated with a small group of Mayan women who live by Lake Atitlán, Guatemala to create a handwoven collection. Service isn't a campaign — it's how I operate.

Embrace Change. We are all going through miniature spiritual rebirths. Every piece I make is a reminder to keep stepping into the next chapter — whatever it holds.
