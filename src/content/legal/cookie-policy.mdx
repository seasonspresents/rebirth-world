## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better browsing experience, and give website operators information about how their site is being used.

## Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in the following ways:

### Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find these options in the "Settings," "Preferences," or "Privacy" section of your browser. Common options include:

- Blocking all cookies
- Accepting only first-party cookies
- Deleting cookies when you close the browser
- Being notified when a cookie is set

Please note that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website, particularly authentication and theme preferences.
