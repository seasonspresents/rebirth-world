# The Complete Ring Sizing Guide

Ordering a ring online can feel risky if you're not sure of your size. This guide covers everything you need to measure accurately at home, convert between sizing systems, and get the perfect fit on your first try.

## How to Measure Your Ring Size at Home

### Method 1: Measure an Existing Ring

If you already own a ring that fits the finger you're sizing for:

1. Place the ring on a flat surface
2. Measure the **inside diameter** in millimeters using a ruler
3. Use the conversion chart below to find your size

### Method 2: The String Method

1. Cut a thin strip of paper or a piece of string
2. Wrap it snugly around the base of the finger you want to size
3. Mark where the paper or string overlaps
4. Lay it flat and measure the length in millimeters
5. This is your **circumference** — use the chart below to convert

### Method 3: Printable Ring Sizer

We include a printable ring sizer with size guide in every order confirmation email. Print it at 100% scale (no scaling or "fit to page"), cut out the sizer, and wrap it around your finger.

## Tips for an Accurate Measurement

### Measure at the Right Time

Your fingers change size throughout the day:

- **Best time:** Late afternoon or evening, when fingers are at their largest
- **Avoid:** Early morning (fingers are slimmest) or right after exercise (swollen)
- **Temperature matters:** Cold weather shrinks fingers, warm weather expands them

### Account for Knuckle Size

If you have large knuckles relative to the base of your finger, measure both the knuckle and the base. Choose a size that's comfortable over the knuckle but doesn't spin freely at the base. When in between sizes, go up.

### Consider the Band Width

Wider bands fit tighter than thin ones. If you're ordering a ring with a band wider than 6mm, consider going up half a size from your measurement.

We offer half sizes for most of our ring designs. If you measure between two whole sizes, we recommend going with the half size rather than rounding up to the next full size.
