# How AI Booking Assistants Actually Work for Tattoo Shops

When people hear "AI assistant," they picture a chatbot that says "I'm sorry, I didn't understand that" every other message. That's not what Satori does.

Here's how a modern AI booking assistant actually handles the day-to-day chaos of a busy tattoo shop.

## It Starts With Your Voice

Before Satori answers a single message, it learns how **you** talk. Your pricing structure. Your style specialties. Your booking policies. Whether you say "sick" or "beautiful" when a client shares a reference photo.

The result is an AI that sounds like your shop — because it was trained on your shop.

## The Four Channels

Tattoo clients reach out everywhere. Satori covers all of them:

### 1. Instagram DMs

This is where most inquiries start. Satori reads incoming DMs, responds with your shop's personality, asks about placement and size, shares pricing ranges, and books appointments — all within the Instagram conversation.

### 2. SMS & Text

Some clients prefer texting. Satori handles SMS the same way — conversational, on-brand, and always responsive. No more "sorry just saw this" texts three days later.

### 3. Voice Calls

When someone calls and you're mid-session, Satori picks up. It answers common questions (hours, pricing, availability), checks your calendar for open slots, and books them in. The caller never knows they're talking to AI.

### 4. Outbound Follow-ups

The real money is in follow-ups. Satori reaches out to clients who went quiet, reminds people about upcoming appointments, and re-engages leads who never booked. This alone recovers thousands in lost revenue.

## What Happens During a Conversation

Here's a typical flow:

1. **Client sends a DM** asking about a floral piece on the forearm
2. **Satori responds** within seconds — asks about size, placement, and whether they have reference images
3. **Client shares a photo** — Satori acknowledges the style and shares a price range
4. **Client wants to book** — Satori checks your calendar, finds available slots, and confirms the appointment
5. **Booking confirmed** — client gets a confirmation, deposit info is shared, and the appointment shows up in your calendar

Total time you spent: **zero minutes.**

## The 60-Second Rule

Research shows that responding within the first minute increases conversion rates by over 300%. After 5 minutes, the odds of booking drop dramatically.

Satori responds in under 60 seconds, 24/7. That's the difference between a booked client and a ghosted DM.

## Not a Chatbot — A Trained Assistant

The key difference between Satori and a generic chatbot:

- **Chatbots** follow rigid scripts and break when someone asks something unexpected
- **Satori** understands context, handles curveballs, and escalates to you when a conversation genuinely needs a human touch

You set the boundaries. Satori handles everything within them.
