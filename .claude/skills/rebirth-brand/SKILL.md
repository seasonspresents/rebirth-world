## Brand Voice Quick Rules

**We sound like:** A grounded skater friend who's also a master craftsman.

**Always:**
- Lead with story, follow with specs
- Use "crafted" not "manufactured"
- Show sustainability, don't preach it
- Reference the community (boards donated by local skaters)
- Use warm, personal tone — Daniel is the brand

**Never:**
- Corporate jargon or salesy language
- "Luxury" (say "premium" sparingly)
- "Eco-friendly" as a lead (show don't tell)
- "Handcrafted artisanal" (too pretentious)
- Generic stock-photo aesthetics
- Template-sounding email copy

## Design System

### Colors
| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary Dark | #1a1a1a | Text, headers, dark backgrounds |
| Primary Light | #f5f0e8 | Backgrounds, light sections |
| Accent 1 | #2a9d8f | CTAs, highlights, links |
| Accent 2 | #e07a3a | Skateboard warmth, secondary CTA |
| Accent 3 | #d4a0a0 | Soft moments, gift guides |
| Neutral | #8a8578 | Body text, secondary info |
| Earth | #5a6b4a | Eco/sustainability callouts |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Clash Display (bold, slightly condensed, -0.03em tracking)
- **Body:** DM Sans (clean, warm, readable)
- **System/Labels:** DM Mono (monospace for numbers, links, technical text)

### UI Patterns
- Grain/noise SVG texture overlay on backgrounds (kills AI-slop feel)
- Framer Motion for scroll-triggered animations and hover states
- Aceternity UI components for hero sections and product showcases
- Natural photography: golden hour, lifestyle > sterile product shots
- Generous whitespace — this isn't a cluttered marketplace

### Manufacturing Specs (for accurate product copy)
- Metal shells: ARZ Steel wholesale ($15 steel / $18 gold-plated)
- Wood: Stabilized Irish bog oak, ancient bog oak, exotic species
- Adhesive: Starbond CA glue system (thin/med/thick + accelerator)
- Surface prep: 91% isopropyl alcohol
- Interior finish: Thin CA glue coat (NOT polyurethane)
- Sizing: Size 9 shell (18.8mm ID) + 0.8mm wood liner → wearable size 7
- Warranty: 1-year on all products
